Things to do in San Juan, Puerto Rico



Las Fiestas de la Calle San Sebastian

Location

  • The San Sebastián Street Festival is a Puerto Rican festival that takes place in the Old San Juan.

Activities

  • Live Music
  • Dancing in every street of Old San Juan
  • Circus performances

Cost

  • Free

Age Group

  • This activity is for any age. Family friendly.


Visit Castillo San Felipe del Morro

Location

  • San Juan, Puerto Rico.

Activities

  • Explore the Forts
  • Go on a Picnic
  • Fly a Kite
  • Walk the Trails

Cost

  • Adults: $10.00 Receipt valid for 24-hours to access both fortifications. Children (15-years and younger): Free

Age Group

  • This activity is for any age. Family friendly.
